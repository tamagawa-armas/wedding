Travel Information for Hawaii Event

Airport

Use the Daniel K. Inouye International Airport in Honolulu (HNL).

Be sure to bring a blue or black pen on your person when flying to Hawaii, as there is a form to fill out mid-flight. Please familiarize yourself with the agricultural restrictions when traveling between Hawaii and the mainland. These rules also apply if you are flying through the mainland but your final destination is elsewhere. These are super important! Hawaii has a unique ecosystem, and cross contamination either way could be devastating to both the environment and the economy.

Ground Transportation on Oahu

The trusted local taxi companies are The Cab (808-422-2222) and Charley's Taxi (808-233-3333).

TheBus is the local public transportation system, but we recommend a car if you want to go most places on the island.

If you are planning to mostly stay in Waikiki, but leave once or twice to explore, consider looking into renting a car from Hui. Similar to Zipcar, you can do short term rentals from Waikiki. Note that the company uses dash cams to monitor driving safety.

If you plan to stay on Oahu for more than just our wedding, we recommend renting a car from the airport.

Notes about rental cars:

  • If you're from the mainland, when you fill gas you might notice that the splash guard and locking lever are missing from the nozzles. This is normal - just manually keep the lever depressed and it will still automatically shut off when your tank is full.
  • For those traveling with children, you can read up on child seat laws here.

Notes about pedestrian laws:

  • It is illegal to be distracted on your phone while crossing the street. When you get to an intersection, be alert of your surroundings and cross with your eyes on the road.
  • You may not enter a crosswalk while the flashing red hand is on. If you don't enter the intersection before then, be patient and wait for the next light.

Hotel in Hawaii

The wedding will be held at the Hilton Hawaiian Village in Waikiki. You can use the coupon code WED to get a discount on rooms (Hawaii residents, contact Sheri for the Kama`aina code), but note that there may be better deals available, especially if you book early. This hotel will be ideal for guests who want a convenient location in relation to the wedding and want to stay in the heart of Waikiki with easy access to the beach and other tourist activities. The wedding venue is connected to the Kalia Tower, but the entire complex is easily accessible from our ceremony and reception area.

For another option, we highly recommend the Ala Moana Hotel. Located less than a mile from our wedding venue and attached to a large outdoor mall with many food options, this hotel is ideal for guests who want a cheaper but still convenient option. This location is walkable to our venue if you tolerate heat and humidity well (Hawaii is warm, even at night), and we recommend walking in groups at night. Note that the sponsored link on Google is not the official hotel website, and we recommend using the link above.

We recommend supporting the local economy and NOT going with an AirBnB-type rental which are primarily owned by people who live out of state.

Note: if you plan on preparing your own food while in Hawaii, be sure to thoroughly wash all locally grown produce that you plan to consume raw. Thoroughly scrub every surface under running water.

Travel Information for California Event

Airport

If you prefer the cheapest possible direct flights, we recommend flying into LAX and renting a car.

If you plan on spending more time in California, we recommend looking into using other nearby airports, such as Long Beach (LGB), John Wayne (SNA, closest to Disneyland), or Burbank (BUR). Flights will generally be more expensive than those coming from LAX, but the TSA lines will be shorter and there will be much less traffic getting to/from the airport.

Ground Transportation

The greater Los Angeles area is known for its lack of public transportation and heavy traffic. We recommend renting a car, but be prepared to sit in traffic and deal with aggressive drivers.

Hotel

We won't have a hotel block, and we haven't personally stayed in hotels in the area. However, the Redac Gateway Hotel and the Miyako Hybrid Hotel are used by Sheri's company so they're probably okay.
