King Taco

This is a popular spot for street tacos. You may want to keep the following Spanish translations in mind when looking at their menu:

  • cabeza : brain or head
  • lengua: tongue
  • buche: throat or stomach

La Taqueria Brand

All of their tacos are good, but the bolsa de churros is...a bag of churros, need we say more?

Amor y Tacos

This Michelin Bib Gourmand (like a Michelin star, but at a lower price point) restaurant has Mexican fusion food.
