In-N-Out

This famous burger spot makes people from California excited. Their standard menu is very good for the price, but their secret menu is also well-known. Our usual order is "two cheese burgers with grilled onions and fries, with one root beer and one lemonade."

Boudin Bakery

Originating in San Francisco, this bakery-turned-restaurant is famous for their clam chowder in a bread bowl. Their sandwiches and mac and cheese are also fantastic.

Din Tai Fung

This dim sum restaurant is famous for their Xiao Long Bao (soup dumplings). If you don't want to spend hours waiting in line, go during an off-time or make a reservation a few weeks in advance.

Urth Caffe

If you're looking for stereotypical California food like avocado toast and gluten-free sandwiches, this is a great spot to try.

Porto's

This famous bakery is known for both their savory and sweet options.
