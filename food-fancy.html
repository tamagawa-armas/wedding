Haleiwa Joe's

If you're looking to enjoy locally-inspired flavors (or a GIANT slab of prime rib) while overlooking the beautiful Koolau mountain range, the Kaneohe location is the place for you! They don't take reservations and have a small parking lot, so if you don't want to wait forever for a table you're best bet is to show up before they open.

We have never been to the Haleiwa location, but presumably it's very good, too.

53 by the Sea

Good food and stunning views of Diamond Head from their panoramic windows.

Yohei Sushi

This (the Dilingham location) is Sheri's family's favorite Japanese restaurant. Sheri's favorite dish is the Yohei special Sushi Zen (it comes with a tiny scoop of ice cream at the end!). The Kahala location is significantly more expensive, so we haven't gone there.
