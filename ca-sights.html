Griffith Park

This famous park includes a zoo, a planetarium, and views of the Hollywood sign.

The Huntington Library and Gardens

This botanical garden in Pasadena is so large, you would have a hard time seeing the whole thing in one day. There's also a play area for children, an art museum, and a library with rotating exhibits and rare collections.
