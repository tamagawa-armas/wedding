Foodland's Poke

Many people consider this grocery store chain to carry the best poke.

Times Supermarket's Poke

This is the grocery store where Sheri's family usually buys their poke (and other groceries). Sheri's favorite are the shoyu and ahi limu. Limu is a type of seaweed, and shoyu is soy sauce.

Wholesale Unlimited

This is the go-to spot for local style snacks. Since you're looking for something savory, we recommend the crack seed, Hurricane Popcorn, or kakimochi (also called arare or mochi crunch).
