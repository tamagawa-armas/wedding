Polynesian Cultural Center

If you want to attend their luau dinner, be sure to reserve your space early!
Come learn about history and culture throughout the Pacific at this world-class interactive cultural museum. Attend live cultural presentations, sample food, make traditional crafts, and much more. If you want to fully experience all of the villages, plan to be here for more than one day. It is about an hour drive from our wedding venue (assuming no traffic), so plan accordingly. There are also available shuttles from Waikiki. You will spend a lot of time outdoors, so wear sunscreen and consider bringing a hat/sunglasses. If you only have one day to spend here, we recommend starting with the villages of Hawaii and Samoa.

Bishop Museum

Learn about the history of Hawaii in a building constructed in part by Andrew's ancestors! You can also check out the cool science exhibits in the other buildings.

Plantation Village

Come learn about how early immigrants to Hawaii lived and worked, including Sheri's ancestors. There are a lot of mosquitoes, so wear bug spray.

Pearl Harbor Aviation Museum

Come see some cool airplanes and learn about Hawaii's role in WW2. It's worth it to go to the top of the control tower. Be sure to check out the actual bullet holes in the Hangar 79 from the attack on Pearl Harbor.
While you're here, also stop by the USS Missouri and the Arizona Memorial.

Iolani Palace

Did you know Hawaii is the only state in the country with royal palaces? Learn about Hawaiian history in this historic building.
