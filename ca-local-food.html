Waka Sakura

This conveyor belt sushi restaurant is located on the second floor of Tokyo Central in Gardena. The Wagyu trio is our favorite dish, but we haven't had anything disappointing here.

Aunty Maile's

One of the best places in "Los Angeles" for (Hawaiian) local food. They serve big portions and also sometimes have other local snacks.

The Local Place

Attached to the King's Hawaiian bakery, this place serves a fantastic local breakfast. Sheri and Andrew frequent this place when they end up at work too early and want to kill time before going to the office.
